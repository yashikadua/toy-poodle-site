Come, Let's discuss Blind spots of
The Toy Poodle!

Toy Poodles are adorable and popular companions, but like any breed, they have their specific characteristics and potential blind spots that owners should be aware of:

Grooming Needs:

Toy Poodles require regular grooming due to their curly coats. Without proper care, their fur can become matted and tangled.

Dental Issues:

They are prone to dental problems, so regular teeth cleaning is essential.

Health Problems:

Like many small breeds, they can be susceptible to certain health issues such as patellar luxation, hip dysplasia, and eye problems.

Anxiety and Separation Issues:

Toy Poodles are highly social and can develop separation anxiety if left alone for long periods.

Exercise Needs:

Despite their small size, they are active and need regular exercise to keep them happy and healthy.
